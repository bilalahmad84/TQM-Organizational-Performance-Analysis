# 📊 TQM & Organizational Performance — SPSS Analysis

> Examining the impact of **Total Quality Management (TQM)** practices on organizational
> performance across financial, business, and effectiveness dimensions.
> Conducted as an independent consultancy project.

---

## 📋 Project Overview

This project investigates how **TQM practices** — including quality policy, employee
involvement, and reward & recognition — influence organizational performance outcomes.
Using **SPSS**, the analysis covers descriptive statistics, frequency distributions, and
linear regression models to quantify the relationship between TQM dimensions and
performance indicators across a sample of **180 respondents**.

---

## 🎯 Research Objectives

- Examine the impact of **Quality Policy** on Financial Performance
- Assess the effect of **Employee Involvement** on Business Performance
- Investigate the role of **Reward & Recognition** on Organizational Effectiveness
- Quantify the moderating role of **Top Management Commitment** across all models

---

## 🔬 Research Design

| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Study Type** | Cross-sectional survey-based study |
| **Sample Size** | 180 respondents |
| **Data Collection** | Likert-scale questionnaire |
| **Statistical Methods** | Descriptive statistics, frequency analysis, linear regression, ANOVA |
| **Software** | SPSS |

---

## 📊 Key Findings

### Model 1 — Quality Policy & Financial Performance
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| R (Correlation) | 0.741 |
| R Square | 0.549 |
| F-statistic | 107.750 |
| Significance | 0.000 |

- **54%** of variation in financial performance explained by quality policy and top management commitment
- Both quality policy and top management commitment **significantly affect** financial performance
- Positive coefficients confirm that **higher quality policy = better financial performance**

---

### Model 2 — Employee Involvement & Business Performance
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| R (Correlation) | 0.993 |
| R Square | 0.986 |
| F-statistic | 6185.200 |
| Significance | 0.000 |

- **98%** of variation in business performance explained by employee involvement and top management commitment
- Extremely high correlation indicates **strong link** between employee involvement and business performance
- Both independent variables statistically significant at **p = 0.000**

---

### Model 3 — Reward & Recognition & Organizational Effectiveness
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| R (Correlation) | 0.993 |
| R Square | 0.987 |
| F-statistic | 6664.482 |
| Significance | 0.000 |

- **98%** of variation in organizational effectiveness explained by reward & recognition and top management commitment
- Strong positive relationship confirms **higher reward & recognition = greater organizational effectiveness**
- Both predictors statistically significant at **p = 0.000**

## 🔄 Analytical Workflow

### 1. 📥 Descriptive Analysis
- Frequency distributions for all Likert-scale items
- Mean comparisons between TQM dimensions and performance outcomes
- Identified close mean values indicating strong relationships

### 2. 📐 Regression Models
- Three separate linear regression models developed
- Each model includes **Top Management Commitment** as a moderating variable
- Reported R, R-Square, ANOVA, and coefficient tables

### 3. 📊 Model Interpretation
- Evaluated model fit using R-Square and F-statistics
- Assessed statistical significance of each predictor
- Interpreted direction and strength of relationships
